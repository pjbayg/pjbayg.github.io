Schedule Generator

This page generates a schedule. It is based on nights rather than days. In general, each night starts in the late afternoon/early evening and continues until the next morning. Satuday is special in that it starts in the morning and extends until the next evening.

Below you can set various parameters for schedules. These are mostly penalties that get applied to schedules for failing to meet certain ideals. E.g. if a Friday night is disconnected from the Saturday then there is (by default) a 2 point penalty applied. A perfect schedule would get a total score of 0 but this is generally impossible.
